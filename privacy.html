Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 29, 2024

1. Introduction

studying.works ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Google User ID

Content You Create

  • Flashcards and study decks
  • Quiz responses and SAT practice answers
  • Community uploads and shared decks
  • Learning profile quiz results
  • Custom flashcard groups and organization

Usage Data

  • Study session data and daily streak tracking
  • Quiz scores, SAT practice progress, and topic mastery
  • BKT (Bayesian Knowledge Tracking) mastery scores
  • IRT (Item Response Theory) ability tracking for SAT
  • Memory game scores and performance
  • Achievement unlocks and progress milestones
  • Feature usage patterns and study mode preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Personalize your study experience
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Enable community features
  • Send important updates
  • Prevent abuse and maintain security

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored using:

  • Firebase Firestore: Account data, community content, shared decks, and cloud-synced progress
  • IndexedDB (Primary): Flashcard decks, study progress, and local data (unlimited capacity)
  • localStorage (Fallback): Study preferences and session data when IndexedDB unavailable
  • Auto-sync: Data syncs to Firebase every 5 minutes and on page unload

All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS with industry-standard security measures.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We share data only in these cases:

  • Community Content: Flashcards you upload to the community are visible to other users
  • Service Providers: Firebase/Google for hosting and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. AI and Your Data

When you use AI flashcard generation, you can choose from multiple providers:

  • Hugging Face (Free): Flan-T5, Mistral-7B, or Llama-3-8B models
  • OpenAI: GPT-3.5 (requires your API key)
  • Claude (Anthropic): Manual prompt option for Claude/ChatGPT/Gemini
  • Your input text is sent to the selected AI provider for processing
  • We don't store AI prompts long-term, only generated flashcards
  • Each provider's privacy policy applies to their AI processing
  • API keys (if provided) are stored locally and not shared

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Delete your account and data at any time
  • Export: Download your flashcards and study data
  • Control: Manage what content you share publicly

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking:

  • Authentication cookies: Keep you signed in
  • Local storage: Save your preferences
  • No third-party tracking: We don't use analytics that track you across sites

9. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while you use the Service
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Community content: May remain visible after deletion unless explicitly removed

10. Children's Privacy

studying.works is designed for students of all ages. If you are under 13, please have a parent or guardian review this policy with you.

11. Security

We protect your data with:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfer
  • Secure authentication via Google
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the new policy. We'll notify users of significant changes.

13. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Contact us through the platform or at the email provided on our website.

Your privacy matters to us.

We're committed to protecting your data and being transparent about how we use it.
